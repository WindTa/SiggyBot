SiggyBot

SiggyBot is a Discord bot that will play a custom audio signature when you join a voice channel.
  1. Click the following link:
    2. https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=657028082468782101&scope=bot&permissions=3165184
  2. Sign into your Discord account and select your server
  3. SiggyBot will now be a part of your server
  1. Find a YouTube video
  2. Pause where you want your audio signature to start
  3. youtubeLink: right-click video, then select Copy video URL at current time
  4. duration: decide how long you want your audio signature to be in seconds
  5. In your discord server, enter youtubeLink and duration into the following command:
    ?intro youtubeLink duration
